Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cast my vote online? +

Casting your vote is simple:

  1. Log in to your iVote account
  2. Select the active election
  3. Review the candidates or options
  4. Make your selection
  5. Confirm your vote

You'll receive a confirmation receipt after your vote is successfully cast.

Is online voting secure? +

Absolutely! iVote employs industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Two-factor authentication
  • Blockchain technology for immutable records
  • Regular security audits

Your vote remains private and secure throughout the entire process.

How do I register as a candidate? +

To register as a candidate:

  1. Click on "Login / Register" in the navigation menu
  2. Create an account or log in to your existing account
  3. Navigate to "Apply as Candidate" section
  4. Complete the application form with your personal, academic, and political information
  5. Submit your application for review

Once your candidacy is approved, you'll be notified and listed in the respective election.
